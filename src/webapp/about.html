Our Story

Moffat Bay Marina has been serving the Seattle boating community for over 50 years. We offer some of the most beautiful scenery, lively local businesses, and a thriving community and culture for you to explore!

Slip Costs

Monthly cost = $10 per foot + $10 for electric power.

Example: A 34 ft boat would cost $350 a month.
